				Str Memory Management
			=====================================
=>str data stored in Main Memory in form of Two Types. They are
		1) Forward (+VE) Indexing
		2) Backward (-VE) Indexing
=>In Forwrd Indexing, Indices  (Indexes) are assigned From Left to Right Associativity from 0 to Len(strobj)-1 .
=>In Backward Indexing Indices are Assigned From Right to Left from -1 to - (len(strobj).
