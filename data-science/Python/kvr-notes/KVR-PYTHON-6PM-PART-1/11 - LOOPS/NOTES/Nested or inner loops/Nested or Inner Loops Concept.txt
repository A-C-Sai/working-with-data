		==============================================
				Nested or Inner Loops Concept
		==============================================
=>The Process of Defining One Loop inside of another is called Nsted or Inner Loop.
=>For Every Iteration Outer Loop, Inner Loop executes Finite Number of Times.
=>We can form Inner Loop in 4 ways. They are
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Syntax-1 :'  for loop in for loop
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
		for  Var name1 in Iterable_Object:  # Outer for Loop
		     ------------------------------
		     ------------------------------
		     for Varname2 in Itera ble_Object2:  # Inner for Loop
		           ----------------------------
			   ----------------------------
		    else:
			   ----------------------------
		 else:
			-------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
		---------------------------
		while(Test Cond):   # Outer While Loop

		     ----------------------
